    Purpose

    My passion and dedication for helping my community were strengthened while growing up in Haiti, where I faced many challenges and witnessed numerous disparities among people and children. Over the years, I have strived to be an advocate for change and continue this work through technology, exemplified by projects like Panther Protect and Taste of Ayiti. Here are some of my achievements.

    ELLA.inc Fellow

    In the summer of 2022, I had the pleasure of participating in the ELLA Inc (Entrepreneurial Ladies Leading with Action) camp at the University of Miami. I met dedicated young women from Latin America who were passionate about advocating for societal change. Together, we created a project and business plan, which we later presented to sponsors and judges.

    All Against Hunger

    In the winter of 2023, my business plan developed at the ELLA Inc camp was published by Plus305. This milestone provided an opportunity to raise awareness about Haiti and discuss important issues facing the country.

    Human Rights

    During my academic journey at College Les Oliviers in Haiti, I participated in creating a Human Rights Children's Book. The book addressed crucial issues such as the right to education and security, in both French and Haitian Creole.
